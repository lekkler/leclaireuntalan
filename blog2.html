MY JOURNEY IN STI ALAMINOS

A journey through my experience

ME IN MY FIRST-YEAR AS STUDENT IN IT

At first, I didn’t know many people in our room, but everyone was kind. The lessons were easy at the beginning, but as time passed, they became more challenging. Still, I gradually adapted, learned a lot, and made many friends along the way.

My first year was filled with fun memories and new experiences that helped me grow.

ME WHEN I JOIN SINGING IDOL

One of my most unexpected yet amazing experiences was joining Singing Idol. I didn’t expect to win, but I ended up placing 2nd! Even though I didn’t have the best voice, I was so happy to have the chance to perform in front of a crowd.

That moment boosted my confidence and became one of my proudest achievements.

SPORTFEST 2024 & 2025

In Sportfest 2024, I was part of the Blue Team under Ma’am Maricel and Ma’am Mayveline. They had high hopes that we would win, especially the graduating HRS team, but overconfidence led to our loss. Despite that, I still received the Mythical 6 award. Then, in Sportfest 2025, we finally became champions! It was exhausting, but worth it. My coaches pushed me hard, even joking (or half-threatening) that I had to win MVP—and I did! It was an intense but incredibly rewarding experience, and I’ll never forget the joy of that victory.

My journey in STI Alaminos has been full of challenges, growth, and unforgettable moments that I will always cherish.

MEMORIES I WANT TO SHARE

STI Alaminos helped me build my self-confidence. Before, I was shy and unsure of myself, but through various activities, performances, and interactions, I learned to believe in my abilities. The people I met—my classmates, friends, and even our amazing instructors—played a big role in shaping who I am today. Some of my happiest memories come from simple moments of laughter and bonding with my classmates, whether during breaks, group projects, or school events. The instructors were also incredibly supportive, pushing us to do our best while making learning fun. Because of STI, I not only gained knowledge but also lifelong memories and the courage to face new challenges with confidence.

This part highlights personal growth, friendships, and appreciation for the instructors, tying everything back to how STI shaped your journey.
