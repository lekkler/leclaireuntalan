MY OJT EXPERIENCE

A journey through my On-The-Job-Training

LEARNING VALUABLE THINGS

During my 320-hour OJT at BFP Alaminos, I gained hands-on experience in client interaction, report preparation (using MS Word and Excel), and office procedures. The personnel were very kind and supportive—they even treated us to free snacks! The friendly environment made learning enjoyable, and I improved my communication and technical skills.

It helped me identify my strengths and areas needing improvement, which I could then focus on during subsequent terms.

MEMORABLE EXPERIENCED & CONTIBUTION

One of the most exciting parts of my OJT was learning how to lower the fire pole—a unique experience I never expected! I also witnessed the transition from manual to online registration, and soon, online payment for renewals. Being part of this modernization process made me feel like I contributed to improving their system.
